About Us

I am a software development enthusiast. I enjoy learning new languages and dabbling in cybersecurity for my personal interests. I mainly code in Python but have experience in Dart, Swift, JavaScript, CSS, HTML, and am currently learning C++.

How did I end up here?

I have always had an interest in computers. When I was younger, I would take apart my computer and put it back together just because I enjoyed doing it. As I got older, I had a couple of friends introduce me to coding, where I have found a passion. Coding to me feels like playing a video game, as long as I keep coding my real-life character would start to strengthen up.
